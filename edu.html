Education

UNC-Chapel Hill

1. As a first-generation college graduate, I started my college career at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. While there, I participated in the band, Black Student Movement (BLS) and was an Omega Psi Phi Pearl inductee.

Community College Of Air Force

2. My next educational journey was after enlistment in the United States Air Force. While enlisted, I received my Community College of the Air Force degree and became an American Society of College Pathology (ASCP) certified Medical Laboratory Technician.

Winston Salem State University

3. Then, with my Community College of the Air Force degree combined with my University of North Carolina degree, I was accepted into the Clinical Laboratory Scientist (CLS) Accelerated program at Winston Salem State University and received my CLS(ASCP)m. “Cum Laude” honors certification degree as a Clinical Laboratory Scientist.

Wake Tech Community College

4. Presently I am finishing my second year as an Information Technology Service and Support student at Wake Tech Community College with plans to complete it in 2027.
